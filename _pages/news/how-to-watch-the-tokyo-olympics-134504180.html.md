<div>NBC Sports announced that Peacock will be home to major events live each morning including womens and mens gymnastics, track & field and basketball. Following controversy over tape delays during previous Olympics, NBC began airing events live in 2012 despite the massive time differences. YouTube TV has also added a medal table for quick reference and its Jump To feature lets you hop around events and specific moments easily. Softball, mens soccer and rowing are set to take place on Thursday with rowing, cycling, tennis and womens water polo scheduled after the Opening Ceremony Friday and into Saturday. The softball and womens soccer competitions begin Wednesday (Tokyo time), but the first games aired Tuesday night on the east coast. BBC One and BBC Two will show live events while an additional livestream will be available on BBC iPlayer. Twitch will also serve up Olympics content, but not live events.</div>
