<div>In the nine months to July, US customs officials seized $62.2 million worth of fake AirPods and other counterfeit wireless headphones, according to data obtained by The Information. Counterfeit headphones aren't exactly new, but having hard numbers on seizures highlight just how pervasive they are. Customs agents have seized phony AirPods on at least a dozen occasions across the US in recent years. Around 360,000 units were confiscated during that timeframe.</div>
