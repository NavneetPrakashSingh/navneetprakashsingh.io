<div>Amazon announced the game's first major delay after taking the unusual step of rolling back the availability of Crucible, its first AAA title. Its release date initially slipped to August 25th, 2020, before Amazon announced a month later it was pushing the game back to spring 2021. It will now arrive about a month later, on September 28th, so that Amazon Game Studios can spend an extra few weeks polishing, fixing bugs and improving stability. We know this isn't the first time we've changed our launch date in pursuit of quality, and that it can be disappointing to wait a bit longer." When Amazon first announced the game, it said it would come out in May 2020. Amazon has delayed New World mere weeks before its previously announced August 31st release date.
