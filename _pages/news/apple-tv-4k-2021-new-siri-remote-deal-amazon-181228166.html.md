<div>The new Siri remote is larger and easier to use, thanks in part to a revamped directional pad that makes controlling the gadget much smoother. Apple gave its TV 4K set-top box some love this year by upgrading the internals and revamping its accompanying Siri remote. The pad is also touch sensitive, so you can swipe between TV show and movie options more easily. The set-top box has a HomeKit hub built in, so you can connect various smart lights, locks and cameras to it. However, Amazon has a new deal that knocks nearly $20 off the 64GB Apple TV 4K, bringing it down to $180. It runs on an A12 Bionic processor and it supports Dolby Vision and HDR video at 60fps, plus features like AirPlay 3 and screen mirroring. But it didn't change the price, which means you'll still pay at least $179 for it.</div>
