All rights reserved.Young Voices MatterThe first step for creating engaged digital citizens is making sure were teaching young people that their contributions and opinions matter. Engaged digital citizens know how to use technology to identify and propose solutions and promote action around causes that are important to them and their communities. But regardless of the outcome, learning how to impact community issues using digital tools is an important skill to develop in and of itself. Fortunately, the digital world gives a wide set of tools that can help change that narrative. Young people need to practice using tech to make a difference now, if they are going to be prepared to lead our society when they grow up. There are many compelling stories of youth who have used Change.org to call attention to issues that matter to them.</div>
