<div>The Munich Animal Welfare Association has taken a unique approach to matching its resident adoptable pets with their new forever homes: putting their profiles on popular dating app, Tinder. "The move comes amid concerns from animal welfare groups that the high rates of adoptions seen during the COVID lockdowns are slowing and could reverse as the pandemic ebbs. The shelter hired a local advertising agency to snap professional headshots for 15 cats and dogs, then posted them with astonishing results. By putting posting the profiles on Tinder, the shelter can potentially connect these pets with motivated adopters.
