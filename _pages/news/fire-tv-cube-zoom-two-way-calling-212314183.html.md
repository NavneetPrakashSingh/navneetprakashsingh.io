<div>The company also suggests mounting the device on the top of your TV set, and that you sit about six to 10 feet away. For the best experience, Amazon recommends a webcam with a 60- to 90-degree field of view that can capture video at 1080p. At the very least, youll want a 720p camera that supports USB Video Class (UVC). Over that same time frame, Amazon has offered two-way video calling between Fire TV Cube and camera-enabled Alexa devices. Starting today, you can use a second-generation Fire TV Cube to take part in two-way Zoom calls. Since last August, youve been able to stream Google Meet calls to Chromecast and other Cast-enabled devices. One thing to note is Amazon recommends against using a 4K-capable camera.</div>
