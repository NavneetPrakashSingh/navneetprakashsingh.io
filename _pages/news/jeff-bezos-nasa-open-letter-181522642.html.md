"This offer is not a deferral, but is an outright and permanent waiver of those payments," Bezos said in the letter. In 2019, Amazon challenged the Department of Defense's decision to award Microsoft its $10 billion Joint Enterprise Defense Infrastructure (JEDI) cloud contract.
