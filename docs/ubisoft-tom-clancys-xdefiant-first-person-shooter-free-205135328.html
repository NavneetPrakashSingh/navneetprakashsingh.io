    Tom Clancy’s XDefiant is a free-to-play, team-based FPS | Engadget

    The first-person multiplayer shooter will pit teams of six characters (or Defiants) against each other across several game modes. The games still fairly early in development, but Ubisofts already inviting the public to try it. The first public PC test starts on August 5th, and you can sign up through the XDefiant website for a chance to take part.Please enter a valid email address Youll be able to modify your setup when you respawn to help you adjust to whats happening in a match. Ubisoft is delving deeper into the Clancyverse with Tom Clancys XDefiant, a free-to-play game it revealed on Monday. The initial set of factions include the Wolves (inspired by Tom Clancys Ghost Recon), Echelon (Splinter Cell) and the Outcasts and Cleaners (The Division).
