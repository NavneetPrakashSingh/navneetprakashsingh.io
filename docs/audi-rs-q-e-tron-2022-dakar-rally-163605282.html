    Audi hopes its off-road hybrid will win the 2022 Dakar Rally | Engadget

    The Volkswagen group's desire to crush records with electrified cars now extends to one of the world's toughest off-road challenges. The RS Q E-Tron relies on an electric drivetrain with two modified Formula E motors, one at each axle. While not a pure EV, it will handle extremely long stages (up to 500 miles) with a significantly reduced emissions footprint. Unlike many EVs, the front and rear axles aren't mechanically connected software handles torque distribution instead. If so, it would be the first electrified vehicle to win the gruelling competition. It expects lessons learned from the car to reach production cars. Autoblog reports that Audi has started testing the RS Q E-Tron, a from-scratch hybrid off-roader it hopes will score overall victory in the 2022 Dakar Rally.
